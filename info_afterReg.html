FAQ

Do I need Equipment? Equipment is not required; however, if you do have it, you can use them since we have option for no equipment or Equipment on the exercise page.
Can I workout everyday? Yes, you can. However, we would recommend you to have at least 2(two) days to rest. Also, please try to make the rest days 2 days in row. Your body needs to rest.
How do I stay motivated? Make sure you have enough sleep, set your plan ahead of time.
